ON'S KITCHEN is...

An authentic Thai restaurant with an extensive menu of home made fresh dishes cooked by owner and head chef "On" Khumchaya. She started cooking Thai food at the age of eight years old for herself and her younger brothers and sister in Khlong Suan, Chachoengsao, Thailand.

On takes deep pride in all of her cooking and we at ON'S KITCHEN feel that it shows when you taste her food.

Thank YOU for all your support!
